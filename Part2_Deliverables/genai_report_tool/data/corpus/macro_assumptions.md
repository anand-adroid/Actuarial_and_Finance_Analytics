The valuation applies the prevailing inflation rate, a prescribed discount rate, and a wage-inflation assumption for care-cost escalation. Reserves are sensitive to these: higher inflation or a lower discount rate increases the present value of future claims and therefore reserves. The report discloses the assumptions and a sensitivity analysis for plus or minus 100 bps moves.
