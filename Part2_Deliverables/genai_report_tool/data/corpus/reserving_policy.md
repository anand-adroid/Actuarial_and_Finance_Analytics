Pure premium (expected loss) is produced by the actuarial pricing models and informs gross premium after expense, profit and risk-margin loadings. Policy reserves cover expected future claims and are signed off by the Appointed Actuary. Predictive models inform pricing and economic capital allocation but do not replace the regulatory reserve calculation.
